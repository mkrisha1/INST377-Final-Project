About CookSmart

CookSmart addresses the everyday challenge of deciding what to cook when faced with dietary restrictions, budget constraints, limited ingredients, or lack of cooking experience. Finding the perfect recipe shouldn't be a stressful, time-consuming process. Our platform simplifies meal planning by providing users with a personalized recipe finder that accommodates individual preferences and needs. Using TheMealDB API, CookSmart allows users to search for recipes by ingredient or meal name and access detailed step-by-step cooking instructions with images. Whether you're a beginner cook looking for guidance or someone managing dietary restrictions, CookSmart streamlines the recipe discovery process and helps you get cooking with confidence.
